About The Place

Nusa Penida is famous for its underwater beauty and exotic natural beauty. The coral reefs in the Nusa Penida area are of the fringing reef type with around 296 types. The area of coral reefs in the waters around Nusa Penida is approximately 1,419 hectares. Nusa Penida has a coastline length of around 77.5 kilometers.
